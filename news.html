"How to Train Your Dragon" Live-Action Trailer Released

The beloved animated franchise "How to Train Your Dragon" is making its way to the big screen in a live-action adaptation, and fans couldn’t be more excited! The first official trailer has dropped, giving us a glimpse into the reimagined world of Berk, with stunning visuals and a closer look at Hiccup, Toothless, and their adventures.

The live-action film, directed by Dean DeBlois, promises to capture the heart and wonder of the original trilogy while bringing a fresh perspective to the story. Set for release in 2025, the trailer has already sparked discussions among fans about its casting and visual effects.

In the upcoming live-action adaptation of “How to Train Your Dragon,” Mason Thames stars as Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III, the young Viking who befriends a dragon. Thames is known for his role in the horror film “The Black Phone,” where he portrayed Finney Blake. His performance in that film was well-received, showcasing his ability to handle complex characters. In “How to Train Your Dragon,” Thames takes on the lead role, bringing Hiccup to life in a new, live-action format.
